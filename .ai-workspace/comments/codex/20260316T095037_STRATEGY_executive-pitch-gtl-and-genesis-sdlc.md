## Summary

GTL turns development methodology into a machine-checkable constraint system. genesis_sdlc applies that system to software delivery so AI builds against a governed specification rather than a prompt trail. The result is faster software change with traceability, measurable convergence, and an audit record of what was built and why.

## Executive Pitch

AI has removed much of the cost of code construction. It has not removed the need to account for what systems do, which business rules they implement, or whether they still conform when they change.

That is the gap GTL and genesis_sdlc close.

GTL is the formal layer. It lets an organisation define a build as a typed graph: what assets exist, how they transform, what evaluators must pass, and which decisions require human approval. Methodology becomes explicit and machine-checkable.

genesis_sdlc is the operating model built on that foundation. It packages a governed software delivery lifecycle where requirements, feature decomposition, design, code, tests, and approvals are linked by explicit convergence rules. Progress is recorded as an event stream. Coverage is computed. Residual work is computed. Completion is computed.

This changes the value proposition of AI in software engineering.

The question is no longer whether an LLM can generate code. It can. The governing question is whether the organisation can prove what the AI built, why it built it, and whether the output conforms to the specification it was meant to satisfy.

genesis_sdlc is the answer at the delivery layer. GTL is the answer at the systems layer.

## Business Implication

- AI output becomes bounded by formal constraints instead of developer prompt skill.
- Delivery status becomes observable as coverage, open gates, and residual delta.
- Requirement to code to test to approval traceability becomes structural, not retrospective.
- A change in business policy or regulation can start from the specification and drive a governed rebuild.
- The methodology itself becomes reusable across projects because it is encoded as a package, not held in team memory.

## Positioning

GTL is the abstraction:

Methodology as an executable constraint system.

genesis_sdlc is the application:

Governed AI software delivery built on that constraint system.

The competitive asset is not prompt quality. The competitive asset is specification quality: how precisely the organisation can state the rules that govern what AI is allowed to build.

## Recommended Action

1. Position GTL as the platform capability: formal, reusable methodology encoding.
2. Position genesis_sdlc as the first productised implementation: governed SDLC for AI-built software.
3. Lead with accountability, change control, and traceability rather than generic productivity claims.
